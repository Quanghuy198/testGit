Full itinerary

Day 1

Ha Noi – Arrival (D)

We will greet you at the International Airport of Ha Noi and take you to your hotel for check-in. Overnight in Ha Noi.
Day 2

Hanoi - Ha Long Bay – overnight on cruise (B/L/D)

Good morning! After breakfast, you will check out and depart Hanoi for Ha Long Bay. This scenic drive will take you through picturesque rice-farming villages of the Red River Delta, where you see every day Vietnamese village life. The trip is approximately three hours with a short stop on the way. Upon arrival at Ha Long, you will board your luxury junk boat for a delicious seafood lunch while the junk starts cruising around hundreds of wonderful limestone formations arising from emerald green water. After lunch, you will have the chance to explore one of the wonderful limestone caves dotted around the bay. Continue cruising to a secluded spot for swimming and sun bathing before dinner. Enjoy another seafood feast for dinner. Overnight on the Junk.
Day 3

Ha Long Bay - Hanoi (B/L)

Wake up early and join the Tai Chi session on the top deck or simply relax and enjoy the sunrise over the bay’s towering islands. Enjoy your breakfast with a cup of coffee or tea and get ready for more exploration today. This morning, you will continue to cruise around the bay to find more secluded areas to either swim or kayak in. The kayaks are lightweight and easy to use. All guests will be issued with water buoyancy vests and the junk will be near us at all times serving as a backup vessel. At midday, you will return to the main harbor before transferring back to Hanoi. In the evening, head to the Ha Noi train station to catch overnight train to Lao Cai. Overnight on train.
